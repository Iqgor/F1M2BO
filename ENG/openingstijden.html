Opening hours

The museum

We are currently temporarily closed. As soon as we can open again you are welcome every day from 9 am to 5 pm, also on all holidays.

Shop, café and gardens

The Rijksmuseum shop and Het Café are open from 9 am to 5 pm. For the time being, these are only accessible with an entrance ticket. Rijks restaurant is open from Wednesday to Sunday for lunch and Tuesday to Sunday for dinner. The Rijksmuseum Gardens are open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
